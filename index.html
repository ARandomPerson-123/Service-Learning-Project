Litter in Baltimore County

Introduction

This project explores the issue of litter in Baltimore County and provides ways to combat it.

What is Litter

Litter is any trash (Examples are Plastic Bottles, Candy Wrappers, Bags of Chips, Aluminum Cans, Napkins, Plastic Soda Cups, etc. ) that people throw on the roads, and litter ultimatley ends up in the ocean.
